Ave Maria College

Ave Maria College is the first Catholic college in Liloy, Zamboanga del Norte. Founded in the historical tradition of Catholic education due to its timeless relevance, this institution promises quality education that leads to the formation of men and women with intellectual and moral capacities to eventually contribute to the development of the town of Liloy and its neighboring communities. Dedicated to the Blessed Virgin Mary, Ave Maria College is managed by top administrators with professional training and experience from leading universities here in the country and abroad. At Ave Maria College, learning takes place both inside and outside the classroom as students, faculty, and staff work together to integrate academic, social, and spiritual components for a vibrant college experience.
